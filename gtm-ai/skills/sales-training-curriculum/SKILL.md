## Deliverables

### 1. Onboarding Curriculum (Week 1-4)
**Week 1: Company & Product Foundation**
- Company history, mission, and culture
- Product deep-dive and hands-on demos
- Target market and ideal customer profile
- Competitive landscape overview

**Week 2: Sales Methodology Training**
- Sales process and stages overview
- Qualification frameworks (BANT, MEDDIC, or custom)
- Discovery question frameworks
- Objection handling foundations

**Week 3: Tools & Systems**
- CRM training and best practices
- Sales enablement platform orientation
- Communication tools and etiquette
- Proposal and quoting systems

**Week 4: Practical Application**
- Role-play scenarios with feedback
- Shadow calls with experienced reps
- Mock presentations and demos
- Certification assessment

### 2. Ongoing Skills Development Modules
**Prospecting Excellence**
- Outbound messaging and cadence strategies
- Social selling techniques
- Referral generation systems
- Event and networking tactics

**Discovery & Qualification Mastery**
- Advanced questioning techniques
- Pain point identification methods
- Stakeholder mapping and multi-threading
- Budget and timeline qualification

**Presentation & Demo Skills**
- Storytelling frameworks for sales
- Demo best practices and customization
- Executive presentation techniques
- Virtual selling optimization

**Negotiation & Closing**
- Negotiation frameworks and tactics
- Closing techniques and timing
- Contract and legal navigation
- Expansion and upsell strategies

### 3. Product & Industry Knowledge
- Regular product update briefings
- Industry trend analysis sessions
- Competitive intelligence updates
- Customer case study reviews

### 4. Coaching & Development Framework
- 1:1 coaching session templates
- Call review and feedback protocols
- Performance improvement plans
- Career development pathways

### 5. Assessment & Certification
- Knowledge assessment templates
- Skill demonstration rubrics
- Certification requirements and process
- Recertification schedules

## Training Delivery Methods
- Live instructor-led sessions
- Self-paced e-learning modules
- Video library and recordings
- Interactive role-play exercises
- Peer learning and mentorship

Provide detailed curriculum outlines, session agendas, training materials, and assessment tools in a structured format ready for implementation.
