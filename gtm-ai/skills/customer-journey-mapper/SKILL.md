⚙️ **COMPREHENSIVE OUTPUT STRUCTURE**

**1. Awareness Stage**
   - **Customer State**: Experiencing {{pain_points}} but may not know solution exists
   - **Goals**: Understand problem, research potential solutions, educate themselves
   - **Questions**: "What's causing this problem?", "Are others experiencing this?", "What solutions exist?"
   - **Touchpoints**: 
     - Organic search (blog posts, guides addressing problems)
     - Social media (thought leadership, industry discussions)
     - Industry events (conferences, webinars, podcasts)
     - Word of mouth (peer recommendations, online reviews)
     - Paid ads (awareness campaigns on {{primary_channels}})
   - **Content Needed**: Educational blog posts, industry reports, thought leadership, problem-focused guides
   - **Pain Points**: Information overload, difficulty articulating problem, lack of budget awareness
   - **Metrics**: Impressions, reach, website traffic, content engagement, brand awareness lift
   - **Optimization**: SEO for problem keywords, social listening and engagement, event sponsorships

**2. Consideration Stage**
   - **Customer State**: Evaluating different solution approaches and vendors
   - **Goals**: Compare options, understand pricing, assess fit for their needs
   - **Questions**: "What type of solution is best?", "Which vendors are credible?", "What will this cost?"
   - **Touchpoints**:
     - Website visits (product pages, pricing, case studies)
     - Demo requests and product tours
     - Comparison content (vs. competitors, buyer's guides)
     - Analyst reports and review sites (G2, Capterra)
     - Sales conversations (discovery calls, needs assessment)
     - Email nurture sequences
   - **Content Needed**: Product demos, case studies, comparison guides, ROI calculators, customer testimonials
   - **Pain Points**: Confusion about differentiation, pricing opacity, long sales cycles, multiple stakeholders
   - **Metrics**: MQLs, demo requests, proposal requests, time in stage, engagement score
   - **Optimization**: Personalized demos for {{target_personas}}, clear pricing information, competitive battlecards

**3. Decision Stage**
   - **Customer State**: Final evaluation, negotiations, internal approvals
   - **Goals**: Validate choice, negotiate terms, gain stakeholder consensus, minimize risk
   - **Questions**: "Can we trust this vendor?", "Is the ROI clear?", "What are the risks?", "Can we get approval?"
   - **Touchpoints**:
     - Sales presentations and business case development
     - Free trials or proof-of-concept projects
     - Reference calls with existing customers
     - Contract negotiations and legal review
     - Executive briefings and sponsor meetings
     - Security and compliance reviews
   - **Content Needed**: Business case templates, security documentation, implementation plans, contract templates
   - **Pain Points**: Risk aversion, internal politics, budget constraints, change management concerns
   - **Metrics**: Conversion rate to close, deal size, sales cycle length, win rate, discount levels
   - **Optimization**: Strong reference program, executive sponsor engagement, flexible terms, implementation assurance

**4. Onboarding Stage**
   - **Customer State**: Learning product, implementing, achieving first value
   - **Goals**: Get set up quickly, understand core features, see results, avoid buyer's remorse
   - **Questions**: "Did we make the right choice?", "How do we get started?", "When will we see value?"
   - **Touchpoints**:
     - Welcome email series and onboarding checklist
     - Product training sessions and certification programs
     - Implementation support and kick-off meetings
     - In-app guidance and tutorials
     - Customer success manager (CSM) check-ins
     - Support tickets and documentation
   - **Content Needed**: Getting started guides, video tutorials, training materials, best practices, templates
   - **Pain Points**: Complexity, time investment, data migration, team training, initial adoption hurdles
   - **Metrics**: Time-to-first-value, activation rate, training completion, support ticket volume, NPS
   - **Optimization**: Streamlined onboarding, proactive CSM engagement, self-service resources, quick wins

**5. Adoption Stage**
   - **Customer State**: Regular usage, expanding to more features/users, integrating into workflows
   - **Goals**: Maximize value, expand usage, achieve business outcomes, become power users
   - **Questions**: "How can we use this more effectively?", "What other features should we adopt?", "How do we scale?"
   - **Touchpoints**:
     - Quarterly business reviews (QBRs) with CSM
     - Product updates and release notes
     - Advanced training and certification programs
     - User community and peer networking
     - In-app messages promoting underutilized features
     - Usage analytics and recommendations
   - **Content Needed**: Advanced guides, use case libraries, integration documentation, optimization tips
   - **Pain Points**: Feature overwhelm, plateau in value, user turnover, integration challenges
   - **Metrics**: Feature adoption rate, DAU/MAU, login frequency, seat utilization, business outcome metrics
   - **Optimization**: Usage-based recommendations, CSM-led expansion conversations, user training programs

**6. Retention Stage**
   - **Customer State**: Evaluating renewal, considering alternatives, measuring ROI
   - **Goals**: Validate continued investment, justify renewal, negotiate terms, consider expansion
   - **Questions**: "Are we getting our money's worth?", "Should we renew or switch?", "Can we get better terms?"
   - **Touchpoints**:
     - Renewal conversations (90 days before)
     - Executive business reviews (EBRs) showing ROI
     - Health scoring and proactive outreach
     - Competitive offers and retention campaigns
     - Contract renegotiations and expansion discussions
     - Customer satisfaction surveys (CSAT, NPS)
   - **Content Needed**: Value realization reports, ROI analysis, customer success stories, product roadmap
   - **Pain Points**: Budget pressure, competitive offers, declining usage, missing features, support frustrations
   - **Metrics**: Renewal rate, gross retention, net retention, churn rate, expansion revenue
   - **Optimization**: Proactive health monitoring, EBRs with clear ROI, competitive retention offers, product roadmap alignment

**7. Expansion Stage**
   - **Customer State**: Adding users, upgrading tiers, buying add-ons, expanding use cases
   - **Goals**: Scale usage, access advanced features, achieve more business value
   - **Questions**: "How can we get more value?", "What else can this do?", "Should we upgrade?"
   - **Touchpoints**:
     - Upsell and cross-sell conversations with sales/CSM
     - Product recommendations based on usage patterns
     - Advanced feature previews and trials
     - Expansion business cases and ROI projections
     - Contract amendments and upgrades
   - **Content Needed**: Tier comparison guides, feature demos, expansion case studies, pricing calculators
   - **Pain Points**: Budget approval, change management, unclear value of upgrades
   - **Metrics**: Expansion revenue, upgrade rate, cross-sell rate, time-to-expand, expansion ARR
   - **Optimization**: Usage-based upgrade prompts, CSM-led expansion plays, clear tier benefits, flexible pricing

**8. Advocacy Stage**
   - **Customer State**: Promoting product, referring peers, participating in community
   - **Goals**: Share success, help peers, gain recognition, influence product direction
   - **Questions**: "How can I help others succeed?", "Can I influence the roadmap?", "Is there recognition for advocacy?"
   - **Touchpoints**:
     - Referral program and rewards
     - Case study and testimonial requests
     - Speaking opportunities (webinars, events, podcasts)
     - Customer advisory board (CAB) participation
     - Reviews and ratings on G2, Capterra, etc.
     - User community engagement (forums, Slack, events)
   - **Content Needed**: Referral program materials, case study templates, speaking opportunities, CAB agendas
   - **Pain Points**: Time investment, unclear benefits, privacy concerns, lack of recognition
   - **Metrics**: NPS, referral rate, review ratings, case study participation, CAB engagement, social mentions
   - **Optimization**: Strong referral incentives, VIP recognition program, exclusive access to product team, advocacy rewards
