**1. Target Account Selection & Tiering**

**2. Buying Committee Mapping**

   - Decision-making process analysis for {{stage}} companies
   - Consensus-building strategies across buying committee

   - Power dynamics and influence pathways
   - Multi-threading strategy to reduce single-point-of-failure risk

**3. Multi-Channel Orchestration**
   - Channel mix optimized for {{primary_channels}} and account preferences
   - Sequencing strategy: awareness → consideration → decision
   - Paid media: LinkedIn ABM ads, display retargeting, programmatic
   - Direct mail: High-impact physical touchpoints with personalization
   - Events: Virtual roundtables, in-person dinners, executive briefings
   - Sales outreach: Coordinated sequences with marketing air cover
   - Content syndication and sponsored placements
   - Channel integration and cross-channel attribution

**4. Personalization Strategy**
   - Account-level personalization (industry, use case, pain points)
   - Persona-level personalization (role, goals, challenges)
   - Individual-level personalization (name, company, recent activity)
   - Dynamic website personalization with {{company_name}} branding
   - Personalized video at scale using account data
   - Custom landing pages and microsites per account tier
   - Sales collateral customization with account logos and data
   - Technology stack: CDP, ABM platform, personalization engine

**5. Content & Messaging Framework**
   - Value proposition tailored to {{industry}} challenges
   - Thought leadership content addressing {{pain_points}}
   - Case studies featuring similar companies ({{competitors}} displacement)
   - ROI calculator and business case templates
   - Interactive demos and product walkthroughs
   - Executive briefing decks and one-pagers
   - Objection handling content for common concerns
   - Content mapping across buying journey and personas

**6. Campaign Cadence & Touchpoint Plan**
   - Week 1-2: Awareness building (thought leadership, paid media)
   - Week 3-4: Value demonstration (case studies, demos)
   - Week 5-6: Consideration (ROI tools, analyst reports)
   - Week 7-8: Decision support (trials, reference calls)
   - Week 9-12: Closing (executive engagement, final objection handling)
   - Sales and marketing touch coordination calendar
   - Frequency capping and contact pressure management

**7. Sales & Marketing Alignment**
   - Weekly account reviews and strategy sessions
   - Shared account plans and opportunity tracking
   - Lead routing and hand-off criteria
   - Activity logging requirements and data hygiene
   - Feedback loops from sales to marketing
   - Success metrics owned by both teams
   - Technology integration (CRM, MAP, ABM platform)
   - Compensation alignment and shared incentives

**8. Measurement & Optimization Framework**
   - Account engagement scoring model
   - Pipeline influence and acceleration metrics
   - Win rate improvement vs. non-ABM accounts
   - Sales cycle reduction measurement
   - Deal size expansion tracking
   - Marketing-sourced vs. influenced revenue
   - Cost per engaged account and cost per opportunity
   - ROI calculation methodology
   - Continuous optimization based on engagement data
