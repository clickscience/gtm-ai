### 6. Objection Handling Library
**Common Objections**

**Price/Budget Objections**
- "It's too expensive"
  - Acknowledge → Clarify → Respond → Redirect
  
- "We don't have budget"
  - Explore timeline, alternatives, ROI justification

**Timing Objections**
- "Not right now"
  - Understand urgency, create compelling event
  
- "We're in contract with competitor"
  - Future planning, relationship building

**Competition Objections**
- "We're talking to [competitor]"
  - Differentiation, unique value, risk factors

**Status Quo Objections**
- "We're fine with current solution"
  - Cost of inaction, future vision, hidden costs
