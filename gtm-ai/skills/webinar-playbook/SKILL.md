# Webinar Playbook

🎯 **OBJECTIVE**
Create a comprehensive webinar strategy for {{company_name}} that attracts {{target_personas}}, educates on {{pain_points}}, positions {{product_description}} as the solution, and converts attendees into qualified leads.

📊 **YOUR BUSINESS CONTEXT**
• Company: {{company_name}} in {{industry}}
• Target Audience: {{target_personas}}
• Topics: Solving {{pain_points}} with {{value_propositions}}
• Product: {{product_description}}
• Competitors: {{competitors}}
• Goals: {{key_goals}}
• Channels: {{primary_channels}}

💡 **KNOWLEDGE BASE INPUTS**
{{knowledge_base}}

⚙️ **COMPREHENSIVE OUTPUT**

**SECTION 1: WEBINAR PLANNING**
Develop compelling webinar topics:
• **Educational:** "How to solve {{pain_points}} in {{industry}}"
• **Product-Focused:** "{{product_description}} deep dive and demo"
• **Thought Leadership:** "Future trends in {{industry}}"
• **Customer Panel:** Success stories and best practices
• **Competitive:** "Why customers switch from {{competitors}}"

Plan 1-2 webinars per month with consistent day/time.

**SECTION 2: PROMOTION STRATEGY (3-4 Weeks Out)**
Multi-channel promotion plan:

**Week 1 (Launch):**
• Landing page with registration form
• Email announcement to database
• Social media posts across {{primary_channels}}
• Organic LinkedIn posts from team

**Week 2 (Build Momentum):**
• Reminder emails to non-registrants
• Paid LinkedIn/Google ads targeting {{target_personas}}
• Partner promotion (if co-hosting)
• Retargeting ads to page visitors

**Week 3 (Final Push):**
• Countdown emails (7 days, 3 days, 1 day)
• Social media reminders
• SMS reminders (if available)
• Team advocacy on LinkedIn

**Day-Of:**
• Morning reminder email
• 1-hour before reminder
• Start on time with waiting room

**SECTION 3: WEBINAR DELIVERY**
60-minute webinar structure:
• **0-5 min:** Welcome, agenda, housekeeping
• **5-10 min:** Speaker intros and credibility
• **10-35 min:** Educational content (80% value, 20% product)
• **35-50 min:** Demo or case study
• **50-55 min:** Q&A
• **55-60 min:** CTA and next steps

Use polls and Q&A to drive engagement.

**SECTION 4: POST-WEBINAR FOLLOW-UP**
Nurture campaign for conversions:
• **Day 1:** Thank you email + recording + slides to attendees
• **Day 1:** "Sorry we missed you" email to no-shows with recording
• **Day 3:** Follow-up email with additional resources
• **Day 7:** Sales outreach to engaged attendees
• **Day 14:** Case study or comparison content
• **Ongoing:** Nurture sequence based on engagement

**SECTION 5: MEASUREMENT & OPTIMIZATION**
Track key metrics:
• Registration rate (visitors to registrants)
• Attendance rate (registrants to attendees)
• Engagement (polls, Q&A, stay time)
• Conversion rate (attendees to demos/trials)
• Pipeline generated and revenue impact

🎯 **DELIVERABLES**
✅ 6-month webinar topic calendar
✅ Landing page template and copy
✅ Multi-channel promotion plan
✅ Presentation template and script
✅ Post-webinar nurture sequences
✅ Metrics dashboard and reporting
