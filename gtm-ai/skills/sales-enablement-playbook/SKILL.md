**SECTION 4: SALES PLAYBOOK DEVELOPMENT**

**1. Discovery Playbook**

**Objective:** Qualify opportunities and understand buyer needs

**Framework:** 
- **Goals:** What are you trying to achieve (tie to {{key_goals}})?
- **Challenges:** What's blocking you (surface {{pain_points}})?
- **Current State:** What are you doing today? How's it working?
- **Decision Process:** Who's involved (map to {{target_personas}})? Timeline?
- **Success Criteria:** What does success look like? How will you measure?

**Discovery Questions by Persona:**
- "What are your top 3 priorities this year?"
- "How are you currently handling [pain point]?"
- "What's the impact if you don't solve this?"
- "Who else is affected by this challenge?"
- "What have you tried before? Why didn't it work?"

**Post-Discovery Actions:**
- Send follow-up email with summary and next steps
- Share relevant case study based on pain points discussed
- Schedule demo tailored to their use case
- Introduce technical resource if needed

---

**2. Demo Playbook**

**Objective:** Show how {{product_description}} solves their specific needs

**Demo Structure (30-45 minutes):**
1. **Recap Discovery (5 min):** "Based on our conversation, you're trying to solve [pain points]"
2. **Set Agenda (2 min):** "I'm going to show you how we address [3 key needs]"
3. **Demo (25 min):** Show {{unique_differentiators}} in action, not feature tour
4. **Handle Questions (8 min):** Answer objections and concerns
5. **Next Steps (5 min):** Mutual close plan or trial/POC

**Demo Do's:**
- DO customize to their industry ({{industry}})
- DO use their terminology and examples
- DO show outcomes, not just features
- DO involve them (let them drive if possible)

**Demo Don'ts:**
- DON'T give generic feature tour
- DON'T talk more than 40% of the time
- DON'T skip discovery recap
- DON'T end without clear next steps

---

**3. Objection Handling Playbook**

**Common Objections & Responses:**

**"It's too expensive"**
- **Probe:** "Compared to what? What are you spending now on [manual process / {{competitors}}]?"
- **Reframe:** "Let's look at total cost of ownership and ROI from {{value_propositions}}"
- **Prove:** Share ROI calculator showing payback in [X months] based on {{average_deal_size}}

**"We're happy with [competitor]"**
- **Probe:** "What do you like about them? What would you change?"
- **Reframe:** "Many customers came from [competitor]. Here's why they switched: [{{unique_differentiators}}]"
- **Prove:** Share competitive battlecard and customer testimonial from switcher

**"Now isn't the right time"**
- **Probe:** "What needs to happen first? When is the right time?"
- **Reframe:** "I understand. Many customers felt the same until they realized the cost of waiting was [opportunity cost]"
- **Prove:** "What if we could show value in [X weeks] with minimal disruption?"

**"We can build this internally"**
- **Probe:** "What would that take in terms of time, resources, opportunity cost?"
- **Reframe:** "Here's what our customers learned: Building takes [X months] and [$Y], plus ongoing maintenance. We deliver value in [Z weeks]."
- **Prove:** Share build vs buy calculator and customer quote from someone who tried building
