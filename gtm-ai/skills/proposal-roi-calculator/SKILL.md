Develop persuasive sales proposals and ROI calculators for {{company_name}} that justify {{average_deal_size}} investments, demonstrate clear value from {{product_description}}, and close deals faster.

• Product: {{product_description}}

• Avg Deal Size: {{average_deal_size}}

• ROI Metrics: Time saved, revenue generated, cost reduction

💡 **KNOWLEDGE BASE INPUTS**
{{knowledge_base}}

⚙️ **COMPREHENSIVE OUTPUT**

**SECTION 1: PROPOSAL STRUCTURE**
15-20 page proposal template:
• **Executive Summary:** Business case in 1 page
• **Situation:** Prospect's current challenges ({{pain_points}})
• **Solution:** How {{product_description}} addresses needs
• **Value Proposition:** Alignment to {{value_propositions}}
• **Implementation Plan:** Timeline and success milestones
• **Pricing & Investment:** Clear breakdown of {{average_deal_size}}
• **ROI Analysis:** Expected returns and payback period
• **Case Studies:** Proof from similar customers
• **Terms & Next Steps:** Contract details and signatures

**SECTION 2: ROI CALCULATOR FRAMEWORK**
Build spreadsheet-based ROI model:

**Cost Inputs:**
• Current solution costs (including {{competitors}})
• Team time spent on manual processes
• Lost revenue due to inefficiency
• Technical debt and maintenance

**Value Outputs:**
• Time savings: [X hours/week] × [$ per hour] × [team size]
• Revenue increase: [Y% improvement] × [current revenue]
• Cost reduction: [Z% savings] on [expense category]
• Risk mitigation: Avoided costs

**ROI Calculation:**
• Total Value - Total Cost = Net Value
• (Net Value / Total Cost) × 100 = ROI%
• Total Cost / Monthly Value = Payback Period (months)

**SECTION 3: PRICING PRESENTATION**
Frame investment strategically:
• Lead with value and outcomes, not price
• Show {{average_deal_size}} vs. cost of doing nothing
• Break down annual cost to monthly or daily
• Compare to {{competitors}} pricing
• Offer payment flexibility (annual vs quarterly)
• Justify pricing tiers with feature value

**SECTION 4: IMPLEMENTATION PLAN**
30-60-90 day roadmap:
• **Days 1-30:** Onboarding, training, initial setup
• **Days 31-60:** Full deployment, workflow integration
• **Days 61-90:** Optimization, expansion, value realization
• Success metrics and milestones
• Support and account management
