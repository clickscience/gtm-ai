**1. Multi-Channel Demand Gen Strategy**
   - **Paid Media** (40% of budget): LinkedIn, Google Search, Display, Retargeting
   - **Content Marketing** (25% of budget): Blog, gated content, SEO, thought leadership
   - **Events** (20% of budget): Webinars, conferences, virtual events, roundtables
   - **Partnerships** (10% of budget): Co-marketing, integration partners, affiliates
   - **Community** (5% of budget): Owned community, Slack groups, forums
   - Channel mix tailored to {{target_personas}} and {{primary_channels}}
   - Budget allocation based on pipeline contribution and CAC efficiency

**2. Lead Scoring Model**
   - **Fit Dimensions** (0-50 points):
     - Company size: Enterprise (50), Mid-market (35), SMB (20)
     - Industry: Target industries get higher scores
     - Role/Title: Decision-maker (50), Influencer (35), User (20)
     - Geography: Primary markets (50), Secondary (35), Tertiary (20)
     - Technology: Uses complementary tools (additional points)
   - **Engagement Dimensions** (0-50 points):
     - Website visits: 5+ pages (20 points), product pages (10 points)
     - Content downloads: High-value (20 points), Medium (10 points)
     - Email engagement: Opens (5 points), Clicks (10 points)
     - Webinar attendance: Live (20 points), Recorded (10 points)
     - Demo request: (30 points)
   - **Scoring Thresholds**:
     - MQL: 70+ points
     - SAL: 80+ points + sales accepted
     - SQL: 90+ points + qualified by sales
   - **Negative Scoring**: Competitors, students, job seekers, unsubscribes

**3. Nurture Sequence Frameworks**
   - **Early-Stage Nurture** (Score 40-69):
     - Email 1 (Day 0): Educational content addressing {{pain_points}}
     - Email 2 (Day 3): Industry insights and trends
     - Email 3 (Day 7): Customer success story
     - Email 4 (Day 14): Product overview and value prop
     - Email 5 (Day 21): Webinar invitation or guide download
     - Email 6 (Day 28): Case study and social proof
     - Email 7 (Day 35): Product comparison and differentiation
     - Goal: Move from awareness to consideration
   - **Mid-Funnel Nurture** (Score 70-79):
     - More product-focused content
     - ROI calculators and business case templates
     - Competitive comparison content
     - Demo and trial offers
     - Goal: Drive demo requests and sales conversations
   - **Bottom-Funnel Nurture** (Score 80+):
     - Highly personalized outreach
     - Reference customer connections
     - Executive engagement
     - Trial extensions and POC support
     - Goal: Accelerate to close

**4. Pipeline Acceleration Tactics**
   - **Speed-to-Lead**: Respond to high-intent leads within 5 minutes
   - **Multi-Threading**: Engage multiple stakeholders in target accounts
   - **Sales-Marketing SLAs**: Define hand-off criteria, response times, feedback loops
   - **Intent Data**: Monitor account-level signals for buying readiness
   - **Retargeting Campaigns**: Re-engage visitors who didn't convert
   - **Account-Based Plays**: Targeted campaigns for high-value accounts
   - **Event Follow-Up**: Rapid follow-up within 24 hours of webinars/conferences
   - **Content Upgrades**: Progressive profiling to learn more about leads

**5. Event Marketing Strategy**
   - **Webinars** (Monthly):
     - Educational topics for {{target_personas}}
     - 60% content, 40% product demonstration
     - Promotion: 3 weeks advance, multi-channel (email, social, ads)
     - Registration goal: 300-500, attendance rate: 40-50%
     - Follow-up: Recording, slides, related content, sales outreach
   - **Virtual Events** (Quarterly):
     - Multi-session conferences or summits
     - Industry experts and customer speakers
     - Interactive elements: polls, Q&A, networking
     - Registration goal: 500-1000, attendance rate: 30-40%
   - **In-Person Events** (Annual):
     - Industry conferences: Booth, speaking, sponsorships
     - Executive dinners and roundtables
     - User conferences and customer appreciation
     - ROI tracking: Leads generated, pipeline influenced, brand awareness

**6. Content Offers & Gating Strategy**

   - **Ungated Content** (No form):

     - Short videos and demos
     - Checklists and tip sheets
