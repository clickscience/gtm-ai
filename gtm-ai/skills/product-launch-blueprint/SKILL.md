⚙️ **COMPREHENSIVE OUTPUT STRUCTURE**

**1. Launch Strategy & Positioning**
   - Product positioning statement addressing {{pain_points}}
   - Differentiation vs. {{competitors}} in {{industry}}
   - Target audience segmentation and prioritization
   - Messaging pillars and key value propositions
   - Pricing and packaging strategy for {{stage}} market
   - Beta program and early adopter strategy
   - Launch tier: Soft launch vs. major announcement vs. big bang

**2. Pre-Launch Phase (8-12 weeks before)**
   - Internal launch team formation and kickoff
   - Product marketing brief and collateral planning
   - Beta program recruitment and management (50-100 users)
   - Early customer research and feedback loops
   - Analyst briefings and industry influencer outreach
   - PR strategy and media list building
   - Sales enablement material development
   - Website and landing page preparation
   - Email list segmentation and warming
   - Teaser campaign on {{primary_channels}}

**3. Launch Week Strategy**
   - L-7 days: Embargo briefings with key media
   - L-5 days: Customer advisory board previews
   - L-3 days: Sales team final training and certification
   - L-1 day: Internal all-hands and hype building
   - Launch Day: Press release distribution (newswire + embargoed contacts)
   - Launch Day: Blog post, social media blitz, email announcement
   - Launch Day: Paid media campaigns go live across {{primary_channels}}
   - Launch Day: Webinar or virtual event with product demo
   - L+1-3 days: Follow-up outreach and media monitoring
   - L+7 days: Week one retrospective and optimization

**4. Multi-Channel GTM Plan**
   - **Owned Media**: Blog posts, email campaigns, website takeover
   - **Earned Media**: PR outreach, analyst coverage, customer stories
   - **Paid Media**: {{primary_channels}} campaigns (LinkedIn, Google, display)
   - **Partner Media**: Co-marketing with partners and integration ecosystem
   - **Events**: Virtual launch event, roadshow, conference presence
   - **Sales Outreach**: Targeted campaigns to high-fit prospects
   - **Community**: Forum announcements, Slack/Discord engagement
   - **Influencer**: Tech influencer seeding and review programs

**5. Content & Creative Plan**
   - Launch announcement and press release
   - Product demo video (2-3 minutes)
   - Explainer videos for each key feature
   - Customer testimonial videos from beta users
   - Case studies showing business impact
   - Infographic highlighting key benefits
   - Competitive comparison one-pager
   - FAQ document addressing common questions
   - Sales deck and leave-behind materials
   - Social media assets (6-10 variations)
   - Paid ad creative (A/B test variants)
   - Email templates for different segments

**6. Sales Enablement Program**
   - Product training: Features, benefits, use cases, demos
   - Competitive battlecards with objection handling
   - Pricing and packaging guidance with discount parameters
   - Discovery question frameworks for {{target_personas}}
   - ROI calculators and business case templates
   - Reference customers and proof points
   - Demo environments and trial provisioning
   - Certification program for sales reps
   - Weekly office hours during launch period

**7. Customer & User Enablement**
   - In-product announcements and tooltips
   - Email onboarding series (5-7 emails)
   - Video tutorials and knowledge base articles
   - Webinar series showcasing use cases
   - Customer advisory board engagement
   - Community forum setup and moderation
   - Support team training and runbooks
   - Feedback collection mechanisms (surveys, NPS)

**8. Post-Launch Momentum (4-8 weeks after)**
   - Weekly performance reviews and optimization
   - Media coverage tracking and amplification
   - Customer success story generation
   - User-generated content campaigns
   - Product hunt or similar community launches
   - Industry award submissions
   - Analyst relations follow-up and demos
   - Sales pipeline review and acceleration tactics
   - Feature adoption tracking and in-app prompts
   - Continuous content production (blogs, videos, case studies)

**9. Success Metrics & Tracking**
   - **Awareness**: Impressions, reach, share of voice, media coverage
   - **Engagement**: Website traffic, demo requests, content downloads
   - **Adoption**: Sign-ups, trials started, activation rate, feature usage
   - **Revenue**: Bookings, pipeline created, conversion rates, ASP
   - **Advocacy**: NPS, reviews, referrals, case study participation
   - **Sales**: Win rate, sales cycle, quota attainment, deal size
   - Dashboard setup and weekly reporting cadence
