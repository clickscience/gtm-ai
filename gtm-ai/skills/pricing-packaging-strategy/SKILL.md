**SECTION 2: PACKAGING & TIER DESIGN**

**Packaging Strategy:**
• **Number of tiers:** Typically 3-4 for {{stage}} companies
  - Fewer tiers: Simpler, but less segmentation
  - More tiers: Capture more value, but confusing

**Tier Structure:**

**Tier 1: Starter/Free**
• **Price:** $0 or low entry point ($50-$200/mo)
• **Target:** Small teams, individual users, trial/evaluation
• **Features:** Core functionality to demonstrate {{value_propositions}}
• **Limits:** Usage caps that encourage upgrade
• **Goal:** Adoption, PLG motion, freemium conversion

**Tier 2: Professional/Growth**
• **Price:** $500-$2,000/mo (optimize around {{average_deal_size}})
• **Target:** Growing teams, primary {{target_personas}} buying motion
• **Features:** Full core product + integrations + priority support
• **Limits:** Generous but designed to encourage Enterprise upgrade
• **Goal:** Revenue optimization, highest volume tier

**Tier 3: Enterprise**
• **Price:** $5,000+/mo or custom pricing
• **Target:** Large organizations, multi-team deployments
• **Features:** Advanced features, security, compliance, SLAs
• **Limits:** Unlimited or very high thresholds
• **Includes:** Dedicated success, custom terms, professional services
• **Goal:** Land large accounts, expansion revenue, high LTV

**Tier 4: Custom/Strategic (Optional)**
• **Price:** Custom, high six-figure+ deals
• **Target:** Strategic accounts, unique requirements
• **Approach:** Consultative, custom contracts, co-innovation

**Feature Allocation:**
• **Core features:** Available in all tiers (demonstrate value)
• **Advanced features:** Held back for Professional+ (differentiation)
• **Enterprise features:** Security, compliance, SSO, SAML (required for big deals)
• **Add-ons:** Professional services, training, integrations (monetize separately)

**Good-Better-Best Psychology:**
• Make middle tier most attractive (anchor pricing)
• "Most popular" badge on target tier
• Decoy pricing to make higher tiers look better value

**SECTION 3: PRICING OPTIMIZATION**

**Pricing Research Methods:**
• **Van Westendorp (Price Sensitivity Meter):** Survey {{target_personas}} on price perceptions
• **Conjoint Analysis:** Test feature/price combinations
• **Competitive Benchmarking:** How do you compare to {{competitors}}?
• **Value-Based Pricing:** Price against ROI/outcome ({{lifetime_value}} realized)

**Pricing Tests:**
• A/B test pricing on website for {{distribution_model}}
• Pilot new pricing with segment of customers
• Grandfathering strategy for existing customers
• Price increase communication playbook

**Discounting Strategy:**
• **When to discount:** Close large deals, annual prepay, competitive displacement
• **Discount guardrails:** Max 20-30% off list, requires approval
• **Non-standard terms:** Extended payment, pilot/POC pricing
• **Partner/reseller pricing:** Tiered discounts based on volume

**Pricing Fences:**
• Prevent tier gaming and arbitrage
• Usage enforcement and overages
• Downgrade friction (require annual commit)
• Feature gating by tier

**SECTION 4: MONETIZATION ACROSS CUSTOMER LIFECYCLE**

**Land Strategies:**
• **Free trial:** 14-30 days, no credit card vs. credit card required
• **Freemium:** Free tier to drive adoption, convert to paid
• **Starter tier:** Low-friction entry point ($50-$200/mo)
• **Annual discounting:** 10-20% off to secure commitment
• **Pilot/POC pricing:** Limited time, limited scope, path to full contract

**Expand Strategies:**
• **Usage-based expansion:** Automatic as customer grows
• **Seat/user expansion:** Add team members as adoption spreads
• **Upsell to higher tier:** When hitting limits or needing features
• **Cross-sell:** Additional products/modules
• **Professional services:** Implementation, training, consulting

**Renewal & Retention:**
• **Auto-renewal:** Default to reduce churn
• **Multi-year discounts:** Lock in customers (10% off year 2, 15% year 3)
• **Price protection:** Guarantee no increase for X years
• **Success-based pricing:** Tie price to outcomes achieved

**Monetization KPIs:**
• Net Revenue Retention (NRR): Target 110-120%+ for {{stage}} SaaS
• Expansion revenue as % of total: Target 30-40%
• Freemium → Paid conversion rate: Target 2-5%
• Annual → Multi-year conversion: Target 20-30%

**SECTION 5: COMPETITIVE PRICING STRATEGY**

**Against {{competitors}}:**
• **Price positioning:** Premium (10-20% higher), parity, or value (10-20% lower)?
• **Value justification:** How do {{unique_differentiators}} justify price?
• **ROI calculator:** Demonstrate LTV {{lifetime_value}} vs. price
• **TCO analysis:** Include switching costs, hidden fees, support costs

**Price Communication:**
• **Transparency:** Public pricing vs. "Contact sales"
  - Pros of public: Trust, inbound qualified leads, PLG enablement
  - Pros of private: Price discrimination, negotiation flexibility
• **For {{distribution_model}}:** Recommend [public/private] because [reason]

**Pricing page best practices:**
• Lead with value propositions {{value_propositions}}
• Clear tier comparison table
• FAQ addressing price objections
• Social proof and ROI testimonials
• Strong CTAs for each tier
