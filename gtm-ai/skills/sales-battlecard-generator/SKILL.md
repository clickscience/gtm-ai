# Sales Battlecard Generator

🎯 **OBJECTIVE**
Develop a comprehensive sales battlecard for {{company_name}} that equips sales reps to confidently compete against {{main_competitor}}, handle objections, and win deals with superior positioning and proof points.

📊 **YOUR BUSINESS CONTEXT**
• Company: {{company_name}}
• Industry: {{industry}}
• Main Competitor: {{main_competitor}}
• Target Personas: {{target_personas}}
• Competitive Advantages: {{competitive_advantages}}
• Common Objections: {{common_objections}}
• Win Themes: {{win_themes}}
• Average Deal Size: {{average_deal_size}}
• Sales Cycle: {{sales_cycle}}

💡 **KNOWLEDGE BASE INPUTS**
{{knowledge_base}}
Reference win/loss analysis, competitive intelligence reports, customer feedback, and sales call recordings.

⚙️ **COMPREHENSIVE OUTPUT STRUCTURE**

**1. Competitive Positioning Overview**
   - **Your Strengths**: What {{company_name}} does better than {{main_competitor}}
   - **Competitor Weaknesses**: Where {{main_competitor}} consistently falls short
   - **When You Win**: Scenarios where {{company_name}} is the clear choice
   - **When You Lose**: Scenarios where {{main_competitor}} has the advantage
   - **Ideal Customer Profile**: Who should choose you over {{main_competitor}}
   - **Positioning Statement**: One-sentence competitive differentiation
   - **Elevator Pitch**: 30-second value prop highlighting competitive edge

**2. Product Comparison Matrix**
   - **Core Features**: Side-by-side comparison of key capabilities
   - **Performance**: Speed, reliability, uptime, scalability benchmarks
   - **Integration Ecosystem**: Native integrations and API capabilities
   - **User Experience**: Interface, mobile app, ease of use ratings
   - **Security & Compliance**: Certifications, data protection, access controls
   - **Support & Services**: Response times, account management, training programs
   - **Pricing Transparency**: Packaging, pricing model, hidden costs
   - **Customer Reviews**: G2, Capterra, TrustRadius ratings and sentiment

**3. Common Objections & Responses**
   - **Price Objection**: "{{main_competitor}} is cheaper"
     - Response: ROI calculation, TCO analysis, value of {{competitive_advantages}}
     - Proof: Customer case studies showing cost savings or revenue lift
   - **Feature Gap**: "You don't have [specific feature]"
     - Response: Alternative approach, roadmap timing, workaround solutions
     - Proof: Customers succeeding without that feature
   - **Implementation Complexity**: "It's too difficult to implement"
     - Response: Implementation process, average time-to-value, customer success support
     - Proof: Implementation case studies, customer testimonials
   - **Security Concerns**: "How secure is your platform?"
     - Response: Security certifications, data encryption, compliance standards
     - Proof: Security documentation, enterprise customer logos
   - **Scale/Reliability**: "Can you handle our volume?"
     - Response: Infrastructure, uptime SLA, enterprise customers at scale
     - Proof: Performance benchmarks, enterprise case studies

**4. Win/Loss Insights & Patterns**
   - **Why We Win Against {{main_competitor}}**
     - Superior {{win_themes}} that matter to {{target_personas}}
     - Better fit for {{industry}} use cases
     - Stronger customer success and support model
     - More flexible pricing and packaging
     - Innovation and product roadmap alignment
   - **Why We Lose to {{main_competitor}}**
     - Price sensitivity in low-budget deals
     - Incumbent advantage and switching costs
     - Feature requirements we don't meet
     - Brand recognition in certain markets
     - Risk aversion and "safe choice" bias
   - **Patterns to Watch**: Deal size, industry, buyer persona, evaluation criteria

**5. Discovery Questions to Surface Competitive Threats**
   - "What other solutions are you currently evaluating?"
   - "What are your must-have requirements vs. nice-to-haves?"
   - "What's driving this decision timeline?"
   - "Who are the key stakeholders and decision-makers?"
   - "What concerns do you have about switching from {{main_competitor}}?"
   - "What would make this an easy decision for you?"
   - "How are you measuring success for this initiative?"
   - "What's your budget and business case approval process?"

**6. Trap-Setting Questions (Highlight Competitor Weaknesses)**
   - "How important is [area where you're stronger] to your success?"
   - "Have you experienced any challenges with [competitor weakness]?"
   - "How do you plan to handle [use case they don't support well]?"
   - "What's your strategy for [future need they're not addressing]?"
   - "How much time are you spending on [manual process you automate]?"
   - "What's your total cost including [hidden fees they charge]?"

**7. Pricing & Negotiation Guidance**
   - **List Pricing**: Standard rates vs. {{main_competitor}}
   - **Discount Authority**: When and how much to discount (10%, 15%, 20% thresholds)
   - **Value Justification**: ROI framework to defend price premium
   - **Deal Approval Process**: Required approvals for non-standard terms
   - **Competitive Displacement Incentive**: Special pricing for switching from {{main_competitor}}
   - **Multi-Year Commitments**: Discounts for longer contracts
   - **Expansion Pricing**: Land and expand strategy
   - **Contract Terms**: Payment schedules, auto-renewal, termination clauses

**8. Customer Proof Points & Case Studies**
   - **Displacement Story**: Customer who switched from {{main_competitor}} to {{company_name}}
     - Before state: Challenges they faced with {{main_competitor}}
     - Why they switched: Key drivers and evaluation criteria
     - After state: Results achieved with {{company_name}}
     - Metrics: Quantifiable improvements (time saved, revenue lift, cost reduction)
   - **Similar Company Success**: Customers in {{industry}} with {{target_personas}}
   - **Enterprise Scale**: Large customers proving reliability and scalability
   - **Fast Implementation**: Customers achieving value quickly
   - **Expansion Stories**: Customers who started small and expanded
   - **Executive Quotes**: Testimonials from C-level sponsors
