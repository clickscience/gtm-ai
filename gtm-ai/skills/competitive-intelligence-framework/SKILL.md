# B2B SaaS Competitive Intelligence Framework

Perform a detailed competitive intelligence analysis for {{company_name}} in the {{industry}} sector, focusing on B2B SaaS competitive landscape.

Competitive Intelligence Methodology:

1. Competitive Landscape Mapping
- Identify 8-10 direct and adjacent competitors in {{industry}}
- Create a comprehensive competitive set including:
  * Direct feature competitors
  * Adjacent market players
  * Emerging startups disrupting the space
  * Potential future competitive threats

2. Digital Marketing & Positioning Analysis
Audit each competitor's digital presence across:
- Website positioning and messaging
- Value proposition evolution
- Targeted buyer personas
- Key differentiators and unique selling points
- Pricing strategy and packaging

Key Analysis Dimensions:
- Messaging clarity and emotional resonance
- Target persona specificity
- Value proposition strength
- Innovation positioning
- Market segment targeting

3. Go-to-Market (GTM) Strategy Breakdown
For each competitor, document:
- Acquisition channels
- Sales motion (product-led vs sales-led)
- Customer engagement strategies
- Free trial or freemium model details
- Partnership and integration ecosystem

4. Product & Feature Competitive Intelligence
- Track feature announcements
- Analyze product update cadence
- Identify emerging product capabilities
- Benchmark against {{company_name}}'s roadmap
- Detect potential competitive advantages or gaps

5. Content & Thought Leadership Tracking
Monitor competitors' content strategies:
- Webinar and event presence
- Thought leadership content
- Technical documentation quality
- Community engagement approaches
- Speaking engagements and industry visibility

6. Pricing and Packaging Intelligence
Detailed comparison of:
- Pricing models
- Tier structures
- Add-on capabilities
- Discounting strategies
- Enterprise vs. SMB positioning

7. Customer Acquisition and Retention Signals
Look for indicators of:
- Customer logos and case studies
- Expansion into new market segments
- Retention and churn signals
- Customer satisfaction indicators
- Growth trajectory

8. Technology and Integration Ecosystem
Analyze:
- Technology stack
- Integration partners
- API capabilities
- Platform extensibility
- Ecosystem development

Reporting Framework:
- Bi-weekly competitive snapshot
- Quarterly deep-dive analysis
- Key trend identification
- Actionable strategic recommendations for {{company_name}}

Deliverable Components:
- Comprehensive markdown report
- Executive summary
- Detailed competitive matrix
- Trend analysis
- Recommended strategic responses

Evaluation Criteria:
- Market positioning
- Product capabilities
- Customer experience
- Innovation potential
- Growth momentum
