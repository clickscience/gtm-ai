**SECTION 1: BRAND POSITIONING FOUNDATION**

**Positioning Statement:**
Develop a clear positioning statement:
• **For** [{{target_personas}}]
• **Who** [have {{pain_points}}]
• **{{company_name}}** is a [category/product type]
• **That** [delivers {{value_propositions}}]
• **Unlike** [{{competitors}}]
• **We** [{{unique_differentiators}}]

**Brand Essence:**
• **Vision:** Where you're going, your long-term impact on {{industry}}
• **Mission:** What you do every day for {{target_personas}}
• **Values:** Core principles that guide your {{team_size}} team
• **Promise:** The commitment you make to customers

**Category Definition:**
• How you define your category in {{industry}}
• Why this category matters (the problem you solve: {{pain_points}})
• How you're different from {{competitors}} in this category
• The future of this category and your role in shaping it

**SECTION 2: MESSAGING ARCHITECTURE**

**Core Message:**
One compelling sentence that captures your essence:
"[{{company_name}}] helps [{{target_personas}}] [achieve {{value_propositions}}] by [{{unique_differentiators}}], unlike [{{competitors}}]."

**Messaging Pillars (3-5 Core Themes):**
For each pillar, develop:
• **Pillar Name:** e.g., "Speed & Efficiency"
• **Description:** Why this matters to {{target_personas}}
• **Proof Points:** Features, metrics, customer stories that support this
• **Connection to {{pain_points}}:** How this pillar addresses their challenges

Example Pillars:
1. **[Pillar 1]:** Built specifically for {{industry}} with deep understanding of {{pain_points}}
2. **[Pillar 2]:** Delivers measurable ROI aligned to {{key_goals}}
3. **[Pillar 3]:** Superior experience with {{brand_voice}} approach
4. **[Pillar 4]:** Proven at scale with customers achieving {{value_propositions}}

**Supporting Messages:**
• Feature-benefit statements for key capabilities
• Value propositions by persona (customize for each of {{target_personas}})
• Objection responses (price, timing, competition)
• Differentiation points vs. {{competitors}}

**SECTION 3: BRAND VOICE & TONE**

**Voice Attributes:**
Define 3-4 voice attributes for {{brand_voice}}:
• **Attribute 1:** [e.g., "Expert but Approachable"]
  - What this means: Clear explanations without jargon
  - What this doesn't mean: Dumbed down or patronizing
• **Attribute 2:** [e.g., "Confident but Humble"]
  - What this means: Sure of our {{unique_differentiators}}
  - What this doesn't mean: Arrogant or dismissive of competitors
• **Attribute 3:** [e.g., "Professional but Warm"]
  - What this means: Business-focused with human connection
  - What this doesn't mean: Stuffy or robotic

**Tone Variations by Context:**
• **Sales conversations:** Consultative, helpful, ROI-focused
• **Support interactions:** Patient, clear, solution-oriented
• **Marketing content:** Engaging, informative, value-driven
• **Social media:** Conversational, authentic, community-focused
• **Leadership content:** Visionary, authoritative, thought-provoking

**Voice Guidelines:**
• **Do:** Use active voice, short sentences, concrete examples
• **Don't:** Use jargon, buzzwords, passive constructions
