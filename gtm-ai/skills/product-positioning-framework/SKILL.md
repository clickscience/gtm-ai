# Product Positioning & Messaging Framework

🎯 **OBJECTIVE**
Develop a comprehensive positioning and messaging framework for {{company_name}} that differentiates {{product_description}} in {{industry}}, resonates with {{target_personas}}, and drives consistent communication across all touchpoints.

📊 **YOUR BUSINESS CONTEXT**
• Company: {{company_name}} ({{stage}} stage)
• Product: {{product_description}}
• Market: {{industry}} in {{geographic_markets}}
• Target Audience: {{target_personas}}
• Competitors: {{competitors}}
• Differentiators: {{unique_differentiators}}
• Value: {{value_propositions}}
• Pain Points: {{pain_points}}
• Brand Voice: {{brand_voice}}

💡 **KNOWLEDGE BASE INPUTS**
{{knowledge_base}}

⚙️ **COMPREHENSIVE OUTPUT**

**SECTION 1: MARKET POSITIONING**
Define where you sit in the market:

**Category Definition:**
• Are you creating a new category or competing in existing?
• How do you describe {{product_description}} in one sentence?
• What category do {{competitors}} play in?
• Position as category leader, challenger, or niche specialist?

**Positioning Statement Template:**
• **For** [{{target_personas}}]
• **Who** [have {{pain_points}}]
• **{{company_name}}** is a [category]
• **That** [delivers {{value_propositions}}]
• **Unlike** [{{competitors}} or alternative solutions]
• **We** [{{unique_differentiators}}]

**Example:**
"For B2B SaaS companies who struggle with complex sales processes, {{company_name}} is a sales enablement platform that automates content management and accelerates deal cycles. Unlike legacy solutions like {{competitors}}, we provide AI-powered recommendations and real-time analytics."

**SECTION 2: CORE MESSAGING PILLARS**
Develop 3-5 core messages supporting positioning:

**Pillar 1: [Problem-Solution]**
• **Message:** "Stop [pain] and start [outcome]"
• **Supporting Points:**
  - Specifically addresses {{pain_points}}
  - Quantifiable impact (time saved, revenue gained)
  - Proof point (case study, data, testimonial)

**Pillar 2: [Differentiation]**
• **Message:** Highlight {{unique_differentiators}}
• **Supporting Points:**
  - Feature comparison vs {{competitors}}
  - Why this matters to {{target_personas}}
  - Customer story of choosing you over competitor

**Pillar 3: [Trust & Credibility]**
• **Message:** Build confidence in {{company_name}}
• **Supporting Points:**
  - Customer count, retention rates, reviews
  - Industry awards, analyst recognition
  - Security, compliance, enterprise-readiness

**Pillar 4: [Value & ROI]**
• **Message:** Deliver {{value_propositions}} with measurable impact
• **Supporting Points:**
  - ROI statistics from customers
  - Time to value compared to {{competitors}}
  - Pricing model advantage ({{product_pricing_model}})

**SECTION 3: VALUE PROPOSITIONS**
Articulate value by persona:

**For [Persona 1: e.g., VP of Sales]:**
• **Primary Value:** "Increase win rates by 30% with AI-powered battle cards"
• **Secondary Values:**
  - Shorten sales cycle from {{sales_cycle_length}} to [target]
  - Improve rep productivity (close more deals)
  - Better forecast accuracy
• **Proof:** "[Customer name] increased win rates from 18% to 24% in Q1"

**For [Persona 2: e.g., Account Executive]:**
• **Primary Value:** "Spend less time searching, more time selling"
• **Secondary Values:**
  - Find right content in seconds
  - Personalize presentations automatically
  - Track engagement analytics
• **Proof:** "Reps save 5 hours/week on content management"

Tailor messaging to what each {{target_personas}} cares about.

**SECTION 4: KEY MESSAGES BY CHANNEL**
Adapt core messaging for different {{primary_channels}}:

**Website Homepage:**
• Hero headline (7-10 words): Clear value prop
• Subheadline (15-20 words): Expand on headline
• CTA: Primary action
• Social proof: Customer logos, testimonial

**LinkedIn Posts:**
• Thought leadership angle
• Address {{pain_points}} without being salesy
• {{brand_voice}} personality
• 1,500 characters max

**Sales Pitch (30 seconds):**
• "We help [{{target_personas}}] solve [{{pain_points}}] with [{{product_description}}]. Unlike {{competitors}}, we [{{unique_differentiators}}]. Customers typically see [outcome] in [timeframe]."

**Email Subject Lines:**
• Problem-focused: "Still struggling with [pain]?"
• Value-focused: "Achieve [outcome] in [time]"
• Social proof: "[Company] increased [metric] by X%"

**SECTION 5: BRAND VOICE & TONE**
Define how you communicate using {{brand_voice}}:

**Voice Attributes:**
• [Attribute 1]: Professional yet approachable
• [Attribute 2]: Data-driven but human
• [Attribute 3]: Confident without arrogance

**Tone Variations:**
• **Product Marketing:** Educational, empowering
• **Sales Content:** Consultative, solutions-focused
• **Customer Success:** Supportive, proactive
• **Thought Leadership:** Insightful, forward-thinking

**Language Guidelines:**
• **Use:** Active voice, specific numbers, customer outcomes
• **Avoid:** Jargon, superlatives without proof, passive voice
• **Example Good:** "Customers save 10 hours/week"
• **Example Bad:** "Revolutionary platform delivers unprecedented efficiency"

**SECTION 6: PROOF POINTS LIBRARY**
Build repository of evidence:
• **Customer Stories:** 5-10 case studies across {{target_personas}}
• **Data Points:** Usage statistics, ROI metrics, adoption rates
• **Third-Party Validation:** Reviews (G2, Capterra), analyst reports
• **Awards & Recognition:** Industry awards, "Best of" lists
• **Media Mentions:** Press coverage, podcast interviews

**SECTION 7: COMPETITIVE MESSAGING**
Handle competitive situations:

**Direct Comparison:**
• Feature-by-feature battle cards
• Win themes emphasizing {{unique_differentiators}}
• Handling "Why not {{competitors}}?" objections

**Indirect Competition (DIY/Manual Processes):**
• Cost of time spent on manual work
• Risk of human error and inconsistency
• Scalability limitations

**SECTION 8: MESSAGING TESTING**
Validate and iterate on messages:
• A/B test headlines on landing pages
• Survey {{target_personas}} for message resonance
• Win/loss interviews to understand what worked
• Monitor engagement metrics by message type
• Quarterly messaging reviews and updates

🎯 **DELIVERABLES**
✅ Positioning statement and strategic narrative
✅ 3-5 core messaging pillars with supporting points
✅ Value propositions by persona ({{target_personas}})
✅ Key messages adapted for each channel
✅ Brand voice & tone guidelines with examples
✅ Proof points library (case studies, data, quotes)
✅ Competitive messaging framework
✅ Messaging testing and optimization plan
