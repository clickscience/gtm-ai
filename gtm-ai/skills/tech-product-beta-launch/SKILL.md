⚙️ **CAMPAIGN FRAMEWORK**

**CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW**

**Target:** Technical innovators and early adopters
**Beta Cohort Size:** [100-500 users]
**Duration:** 8-week beta period
**Objective:** Gather feedback, create testimonials, build community, generate launch momentum

**BETA PROGRAM POSITIONING**

**Core Messaging:**
"Shape the future of [product category]. Join {{company_name}}'s exclusive beta and help us build the product you've been waiting for."

**Beta Benefits:**
- Exclusive early access before general availability
- Direct line to product team for feedback and requests
- Discounted pricing locked in for early believers
- Recognition in launch materials (if desired)
- Community access with other forward-thinking professionals

**CAMPAIGN TACTICS**

**Week 1-2: Beta Announcement**
- Landing page with waitlist signup
- Founder video explaining vision and beta opportunity
- Social media announcement across all channels
- Email to existing audience and newsletter subscribers

**Week 3-4: Recruitment Push**
- Paid advertising targeting technical audiences
- Outreach to relevant communities and forums
- Influencer seeding (tech reviewers, industry experts)
- Partner referral program

**Week 5-8: Beta Program Execution**
- Onboard beta users in cohorts
- Weekly product updates and feedback calls
- Private Slack/Discord community for beta users
- Feature request voting and prioritization
- Bug bounty or feedback incentive program

**POST-BETA: Launch Preparation**
- Gather testimonials from beta users
- Create case studies from successful implementations
- Build launch content from beta learnings
- Transition beta users to paid plans

📋 **CAMPAIGN DELIVERABLES**

✅ **Landing Page:** Beta signup with waitlist management  
✅ **Community:** Private Slack/Discord for beta users  
✅ **Content:** Founder video, product vision blog post  
✅ **Feedback System:** Survey tools, feature voting  
✅ **Recognition:** Beta user appreciation program  
✅ **Launch Assets:** Testimonials, case studies from beta
