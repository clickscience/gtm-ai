# Account-Based Marketing (ABM) Strategy

🎯 **OBJECTIVE**
Develop a comprehensive Account-Based Marketing strategy for {{company_name}} to identify, engage, and convert high-value target accounts in {{industry}} through hyper-personalized, multi-channel campaigns aligned to your {{sales_cycle_length}} sales cycle.

📊 **YOUR BUSINESS CONTEXT**
• Company: {{company_name}} ({{stage}} stage, {{team_size}} team)
• Product: {{product_description}}
• Target Accounts: {{target_personas}} at companies in {{geographic_markets}}
• Avg Deal Size: {{average_deal_size}} | Sales Cycle: {{sales_cycle_length}}
• Value Props: {{value_propositions}}
• Pain Points: {{pain_points}}
• Competitors: {{competitors}}
• Budget: {{monthly_marketing_budget}}

💡 **KNOWLEDGE BASE INPUTS**
{{knowledge_base}}

⚙️ **COMPREHENSIVE OUTPUT**

**SECTION 1: TARGET ACCOUNT SELECTION**

**Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) for ABM:**
• Company size (revenue, employees) aligned to {{average_deal_size}}
• Industry verticals within {{industry}}
• Geographic focus in {{geographic_markets}}
• Technology stack (using {{tech_stack}} as signals)
• Growth signals (funding, expansion, hiring)

**Account Tiering:**
• **Tier 1 (Strategic):** 10-20 accounts, highest value, full 1:1 personalization
• **Tier 2 (Target):** 50-100 accounts, high value, 1:few personalization
• **Tier 3 (Watch List):** 200+ accounts, good fit, 1:many programs

**Account Selection Criteria:**
• Likelihood to buy (intent signals, fit score)
• Potential deal size and LTV {{lifetime_value}}
• Strategic importance (reference potential, market leadership)
• Existing relationships or warm introductions

**SECTION 2: ACCOUNT RESEARCH & INSIGHTS**

**Deep Account Intelligence:**
• Business priorities and initiatives (from earnings calls, press releases)
• Key decision makers mapped to {{target_personas}}
• Technology stack and integration requirements
• Pain points specific to their business (matching {{pain_points}})
• Competitive solutions they currently use ({{competitors}})
• Buying committee structure and influence map

**Persona Mapping:**
• Identify 5-7 key stakeholders per account
• Map personas to {{target_personas}} framework
• Research individual backgrounds, interests, content preferences
• LinkedIn, Twitter, industry event participation

**Trigger Events:**
• New executive hires (VP Sales, CMO, CTO)
• Funding rounds or acquisitions
• Product launches or market expansions
• Negative reviews of competitor products
• Budget cycles and fiscal year timing

**SECTION 3: PERSONALIZED ENGAGEMENT STRATEGIES**

**Tier 1 (Strategic) Engagement:**
• Executive gifting and direct mail campaigns
• Custom thought leadership content (whitepapers, research)
• Personalized video messages from leadership
• VIP event invitations (dinners, roundtables, conferences)
• Dedicated landing pages and microsites
• Account-specific demos highlighting relevant {{value_propositions}}

**Tier 2 (Target) Engagement:**
• Industry-specific email sequences
• Personalized webinars addressing {{pain_points}}
• Targeted social media advertising
• Case studies from similar companies
• Sales development rep (SDR) outreach with account context

**Tier 3 (Watch List) Engagement:**
• Vertical-focused content campaigns
• Broad-based digital advertising
• General nurture email tracks
• Retargeting campaigns

**Multi-Channel Orchestration:**
Coordinate touchpoints across {{primary_channels}}:
• **Week 1:** Personalized email from AE + LinkedIn connection requests
• **Week 2:** Targeted LinkedIn/Google ads + direct mail arrival
• **Week 3:** Phone call + personalized video + content offer
• **Week 4:** Event invitation or executive briefing
• **Ongoing:** Nurture sequence, social engagement, intent monitoring

**SECTION 4: CONTENT & MESSAGING PERSONALIZATION**

**Account-Specific Messaging:**
• Reference their company, industry, and specific challenges
• Highlight {{unique_differentiators}} vs. their current solution ({{competitors}})
• Showcase ROI relevant to their business model
• Use their language and terminology

**Personalized Content Assets:**
• Custom pitch decks with account name, logo, industry data
• ROI calculators pre-populated with their metrics
• Case studies from similar companies in {{industry}}
• Competitive comparisons if using {{competitors}}

**Sales Enablement:**
• Account profiles with research summary
• Personalized talk tracks for each persona
• Account-specific battle cards
• Relationship maps and engagement history

**SECTION 5: MEASUREMENT & OPTIMIZATION**

**ABM Metrics:**
• **Account Engagement:** % of target accounts engaged, contacts reached per account
• **Pipeline Metrics:** Opportunities created, pipeline value, conversion rates
• **Velocity:** Time from first touch to opportunity to close vs. {{sales_cycle_length}}
• **Win Rate:** Close rate on ABM accounts vs. non-ABM
• **Deal Size:** Average deal size on ABM accounts vs. {{average_deal_size}}

**Account Scoring:**
• Engagement score (email opens, content downloads, ad clicks, website visits)
• Intent score (G2 research, competitor comparison, pricing page views)
• Relationship score (number of contacts, seniority of engaged personas)

**Optimization:**
• Monthly account plan reviews with sales team
• Win/loss analysis on ABM accounts
• Content performance and personalization effectiveness
• Channel mix optimization
• Expansion from successful accounts to similar profiles

**ROI Tracking:**
• Marketing spend allocated to ABM (% of {{monthly_marketing_budget}})
• Pipeline and revenue attributed to ABM vs. other programs
• CAC for ABM accounts vs. {{customer_acquisition_cost}} average
• LTV of ABM customers vs. {{lifetime_value}} average
