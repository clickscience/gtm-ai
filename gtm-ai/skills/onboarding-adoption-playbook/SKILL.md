# Customer Onboarding & Adoption Playbook

🎯 **OBJECTIVE**
Create a structured customer onboarding and adoption playbook for {{company_name}} that accelerates time-to-value, drives feature adoption of {{product_description}}, and sets the foundation for long-term success and expansion.

📊 **YOUR BUSINESS CONTEXT**
• Company: {{company_name}} in {{industry}}
• Product: {{product_description}}
• Customer Segments: {{target_personas}}
• Value Delivered: {{value_propositions}}
• Success Metrics: Solving {{pain_points}} and achieving {{key_goals}}
• Avg Deal Size: {{average_deal_size}}

💡 **KNOWLEDGE BASE INPUTS**
{{knowledge_base}}

⚙️ **COMPREHENSIVE OUTPUT**

**SECTION 1: ONBOARDING FRAMEWORK**
Structure first 90 days into phases:

**Phase 1: Kickoff & Setup (Days 1-14)**
**Goals:** Account setup, team training, initial configuration
• Day 1: Welcome email and kickoff call scheduled
• Day 2-3: Account provisioning and admin setup
• Day 5: Kickoff call - goals, timeline, success criteria
• Day 7: Initial training session on core features
• Day 10: First check-in, address questions
• Day 14: Setup complete, ready for real use

**Deliverables:** Onboarding plan, team access, configuration complete

**Phase 2: Adoption & Value (Days 15-60)**
**Goals:** Drive usage, achieve first wins, build habits
• Week 3: Feature deep-dive training sessions
• Week 4: First "quick win" or use case completed
• Week 5-6: Expand usage to more team members
• Week 7: Mid-onboarding check-in, health score review
• Week 8: Advanced features training

**Success Metrics:**
• [X]% of team actively using product weekly
• [Y] key actions completed (addressing {{pain_points}})
• First measurable outcome achieved (time saved, revenue generated)

**Phase 3: Optimization & Expansion (Days 61-90)**
**Goals:** Optimize workflows, identify expansion opportunities
• Week 10: Review performance data, identify optimization areas
• Week 11: Share best practices from similar customers
• Week 12: Quarterly Business Review (QBR)
• Week 13: Expansion discussion (additional users, features, use cases)

**Deliverables:** QBR deck, expansion proposal, success story (if applicable)

**SECTION 2: CUSTOMER SUCCESS PLAYBOOK**
Ongoing engagement post-onboarding:

**Regular Touchpoints:**
• Weekly (Weeks 1-4): Check-ins and support
• Bi-weekly (Weeks 5-12): Progress reviews
• Monthly (Ongoing): Business reviews and optimization
• Quarterly (Ongoing): Executive QBRs, renewal discussions

**Health Score Monitoring:**
Track customer health with:
• **Product Usage:** Login frequency, feature adoption depth
• **Engagement:** Response rates, meeting attendance
• **Business Outcomes:** Progress toward {{key_goals}}
• **Relationship:** NPS score, executive sponsorship strength

**Risk Mitigation:**
• Yellow flag: Usage declining, missing meetings
• Red flag: Executive sponsor left, considering {{competitors}}
• Intervention: Executive outreach, success planning, renewal campaign

**SECTION 3: ENABLEMENT RESOURCES**
Create self-service resources:
• **Knowledge Base:** Help docs, tutorials, FAQs
• **Video Library:** Product tours, how-tos, best practices
• **Customer Community:** Forum, user groups, Slack/Discord
• **Office Hours:** Weekly drop-in sessions for Q&A
• **Certification Program:** Power user training and badges

**SECTION 4: EXPANSION TRIGGERS**
Identify expansion opportunities:
• Adding more users/seats
• Unlocking premium features
• Expanding to new use cases
• Cross-selling complementary products
• Annual to multi-year commitments

**Expansion Signals:**
• High usage and engagement
• Executive sponsor enthusiastic
• Achieving strong ROI
• Team growing (hiring more {{target_personas}})
• Asking about advanced features

**SECTION 5: SUCCESS MEASUREMENT**
Track onboarding effectiveness:
• **Time to First Value:** Days until first outcome achieved
• **Time to Full Adoption:** Days until [X]% of team active weekly
• **Onboarding Completion Rate:** % completing all phases
• **90-Day Health Score:** Green/Yellow/Red distribution
• **Retention:** Churn rate for customers <6 months old
• **NPS:** Satisfaction score post-onboarding
